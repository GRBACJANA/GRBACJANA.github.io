Calypso

This project is created to promote Calypso font foundery. Set of promotional materials contain Poster: Size B3 1/2 Sided. Folded Booklet: Folded down B3 sheet Format and binding to be decided by designer This printed matter is to be distributed at the design seminars and conferences and also supplied with the purchase of the font as a gift to the purchasing designer.

Jarno Lukkarila became devoted to type design after graduating from Type and Media in The Hague, and assisting at Büro Petr van Blokland + Claudia Mens in 2001 Having studied at Aalto University in Helsinki, he began an independent type foundry in 2006. With some encouraging push from customers and font users it has gradually evolved.
